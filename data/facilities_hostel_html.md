## BOYS HOSTEL

#### Facilities Provided At Boys Hostel

* Well-equipped rooms for Single/ Double occupancy with attached/ common bathrooms having hot water supply.
* Furniture is provided for each inmate with one Cot, Chair, Amirah & Reading table.
* Two spacious dining halls for comfortable dinning.
* Vegetarian Mess menu consists of BREAKFAST, LUNCH, EVENING SNACKS & SPECIAL DINNER comprising of both north and south Indian food items as decided by Mess Committee consisting of students.
* Free Laundry facility (Only outfits).
* Cloth ironing services at subsidized rates.
* Spacious recreation halls with LED TV, Newspaper and magazines available in all the hostel blocks.
* Indoor and outdoor games facility.
* Night canteen facility with light refreshments up to 11PM.
* Health center with a senior Doctor for consultation and treatment available from Monday to Saturday between 5PM to 7PM. Medicines are supplied free of cost. In any emergency, the inmates will be referred to Ramaiah Hospital, which is easily accessible.
* Round the clock security with additional staff at the Fresher’s block occupied by first year students.
* Guest room for visiting parents on chargeable basis subjected to availability.
* Free Wi- Fi facility.
* CCTV Camera surveillance round the clock in all hostel blocks.
  
  
#### Boys Hostel Rules and Regulations

* Only bonafide students of RIT will be given accommodation in the hostel.
* The management of the MSR Hostels (Engg) vests with the Gokula Education Foundation Trust under the direction and control of the Principal, RIT through the Warden and the Officials appointed by him.
* The students admitted to RIT, requiring hostel accommodation should pay the hostel and mess deposits, room rent and other hostel charges in **full for the academic year**  at the time of joining the hostel. **Academic year ends on the last day of exam of the even semester in that year.**
* **All those admitted to the hostel should occupy the rooms allotted to them and shall give an acknowledgment for the furniture and fittings handed over to their charge.** At the time of leaving the hostel during the course or after completion of the course, they will be required to hand over all the items to the hostel office. Fines will be levied for all damages thereto, if any.
* **Extra furniture or electrical fittings such as Iron box, kettle, water heating coils, speakers etc… are not allowed in the rooms. If found, such items will be confiscated and fines will be levied.**
* Change of Rooms / Staying in rooms other than the allotted one is not allowed without prior permission from the warden.
* **Cooking of any type of food in the rooms is strictly prohibited. Outside food (by Swiggy / Zomato etc..) is not allowed into the hostel rooms.**
* Students shall not keep any valuables and objectionable articles in the rooms. **Students shall not write or display slogans / ugly posters / names / flags / objectionable photographs etc. in the hostel rooms or anywhere in the premises.**
* Hostel inmates found indulging in unlawful activities, damaging of any property of the hostel such as electrical and plumbing fittings, furniture etc.. are liable to be expelled from the hostel including forfeiture of their entire deposits.
* **Students should not assemble in the rooms in large groups for chatting / playing / partying / birthday celebrations etc.** If found, strict disciplinary action will be initiated including expelling from the hostel.
* **ostel inmates should avoid causing disturbance to other residents in any manner whatsoever.** It is important that absolute silence is maintained after 10PM. Consumption of Alcoholic beverages, Drugs, smoking of Cigarettes, Non-vegetarian food etc., in the hostel premises is completely banned. If found indulging in such activities, students are liable for expelling from hostel including forfeiture of their entire deposits.
* **Inmates should exercise all possible economy in the use of water and electricity.**
* Students are required to bring their own mattresses and other requirements. Though watch and ward arrangements are provided, the RIT shall not be responsible for any loss or damage to the things belonging to the residents of the hostel. The occupants will get their own lock and key for locking the room and will be responsible for the safety of their belongings.
* Members who expect to be away from the hostel for short periods exceeding 24 hours shall inform the Warden of their absence indicating the probable time of their return. Absence from the hostel without permission for more than a month may entitle cancellation of the accommodation and the adjustment of the deposit towards dues. Notice for the intention to vacate a room must be given in writing in the prescribed form kept in the hostel office. Change of room, except according to the allotment made by the authority, is not permitted.
* Students who have been allotted new rooms will have to shift to the new accommodation and hand over the possession of old room or cancel the new allotment in writing. Keeping both rooms beyond the stipulated period will be viewed seriously and attracts penal fee at guest rates i.e., Rs. 300/- per day (for having kept both the rooms in his name, till the possession of one of the rooms is handed over to the hostel authorities.
* The security deposit is refunded only when the student leaves the hostel. This deposit is subject to deduct the cost of any loss / damage to the furniture & fittings. The Hostel charges may be revised from time to time if necessary.
* Students shall vacate the hostel as soon as the academic year ends or on completion of requirements for their degree. Their stay in the hostel beyond the stipulated time without prior permission from the Warden shall be treated as unauthorized stay and action will be taken to take possession of the seat/room in addition to charging room rent of Rs.300/- per day.

  
#### Mess Rules

* Every boarder shall see that congenial atmosphere is maintained in dining hall to enable the staff to do their job effectively. No boarder shall enter into quarrel or unnecessary discussion with staff or co- boarder.
* Outside food is not allowed into the dining hall/Hostel premises, except the items purchased from the stores situated within premises.
* Service in the mess shall be as per scheduled timings only.
* There shall be no service to the rooms and food will not be preserved for late comers.
* The menu so specified by mess committee will be duly approved by Warden and Dy Warden, to have effective control over the cost.
* The Mess Committee will act as an advisory body and report to Warden about quality of food and on general cleanliness in and around the mess. The Committee will also bring to the notice of warden about the quality of service being given by the mess staff.
* No Mess leave will be entertained to inmates whatsoever the reason may be. Only for emergency cases with genuine reason leave shall be sanctioned by Hostel Authority subject to produce of proof.
* The items being prepared are as per the menu finalized by the Mess Committee. Any change in the menu shall be brought to the notice of the Warden for taking necessary action. In case of any disagreement, the decision given by Warden shall be final and binding.
* THostel boarders should not bring other students into the mess for food. If found, fine will be levied to hostel inmates.

#### Refund policy

Such hostel students who wish to vacate the hostel during the academic session are eligible for refund as per the guidelines detailed below. 

  
| Timeframe                                                      | Eligible refund                                                                  |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| After admission, before occupying the hostel room              | 100% refund (Only admission fee of Rs. 1,000 shall be deducted).                 |
| Vacating within One month from the date of joining the hostel  | 75% of rent and maintenance charges + balance of mess advance + security deposit |
| Vacating within Two months from the date of joining the hostel | 50% of rent and maintenance charges + balance of mess advance + security deposit |
| Vacating after Two months from the date of joining the hostel  | Only Security deposit                                                            |
