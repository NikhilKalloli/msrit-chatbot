[![MOOC_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/d37d19_cc7646b9b797483385eb14c080f615db~mv2.jpg/v1/crop/x_6,y_6,w_759,h_570/fill/w_217,h_160,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/MOOC_edited.jpg)](https://www.msritlibrary.org/open-online-courses)

#### Open Online Courses

A massive Open Online Courses is an online course platform aimed at unlimited participation & open access via the web. In addition to traditional course materials, such as filmed lectures, readings, and problem sets, many MOOCs provide interactive courses with user forums.

[![national-digital-library-of-india.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/438beb_f7f8d228bec844e093e4502c6987b147~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/national-digital-library-of-india.jpg)](https://ndl.iitkgp.ac.in/)

#### National Digital Library

The National Digital library of India is a virtual repository of learning resources which is not only just a repository with a search/browse facilities but also provides a host of services containing textbooks, articles, videos, audio books, lectures, simulations, fiction for user community.

[![e learning.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/d37d19_66f50afe9d524b42bfd861f19b092655~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_25,y_0,w_103,h_77/fill/w_144,h_106,al_c,lg_1,q_85,enc_avif,quality_auto/e%20learning.png)](http://elearning.vtu.ac.in/)

#### VTU e-Learning Centre

The VTU e-Learning Centre was established in August 2003 in Mysuru with the main aim of facilitating distance education and training to the students and faculty of VTU through satellite and web.

[![Khan.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/438beb_225595fbedf848a8bb471c0604b705bd~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Khan.png)](https://www.khanacademy.org/)

#### KHAN Academy

Khan Academy is an American non-profit educational organization created in 2008 by Salman Khan. Its goal is creating a set of online tools that help educate students. The organization produces short lessons in the form of videos. Its website also includes supplementary practice exercises.

[![MHRD.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/d37d19_3bf67c822e9545b2932a4721f23d5ae8~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_0,y_28,w_225,h_169/fill/w_217,h_160,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/MHRD.png)](https://www.msritlibrary.org/mhrd-ict-initiatives)

#### ICT Initiatives of MHRD

ICT Initiatives of MHRD Unique ideas, concepts and information without duplication. 

[![refseek.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/438beb_7d7ee678530441a3a9b8fb8024e28a83~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,lg_1,q_80,enc_avif,quality_auto/refseek.jpg)](https://www.refseek.com/directory/)

#### RefSeek

RefSeek is an academic search engine designed specifically for students and researchers. RefSeek searches more than one billion documents, including web pages, books, encyclopedias, journals, and newspapers.

[![E books.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/d37d19_83920d2346c34e2fbb804525ec655dca~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/E%20books.jpg)](http://172.1.22.22:8080/#library%5Fid=Calibre%5FLibrary&panel=book%5Flist)

#### Calibre 

10,000+ e-Books are available on Calibre \- eBook Management platform in MSRIT Library. These e-Books can be accessed within the campus only.

[![India.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/438beb_d91f7f90e11149629ac54db114f0b96b~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/India.jpg)](https://www.india.gov.in/)

#### National Portal of India

It presents information resources and online services from government sources, accessible from a single point. The portal has been developed as a Mission Mode Project under the National E-Governance Plan of the government.

[![NPTEL.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/d37d19_187c44b2e8904edb8ef2f8965ada7cc2~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/NPTEL.png)](https://archive.nptel.ac.in/course.html)

#### NPTEL Course Materials

Largest online repository in the world of courses in engineering, basic sciences and selected humanities and social sciences subjects.

[![BIS.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/438beb_6219ea1f4a674324a5e968659149986e~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_221,h_164,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/BIS.png)](https://www.bis.gov.in/)

#### BIS

BIS is the National Standard Body of India established under the BIS Act 2016 for the harmonious development of the activities of standardization, marking and quality certification of goods and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.
